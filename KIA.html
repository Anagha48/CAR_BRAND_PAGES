Liability Coverage

Liability coverage is mandatory in Canada and protects you if you’re found at fault in an accident. This coverage ensures that legal expenses and third-party damages are covered, keeping you financially secure while meeting provincial requirements for Kia insurance.

Collision Coverage

Kia vehicles are known for their durability and safety, but accidents can still happen. Collision coverage helps pay for repair or replacement costs if your Kia is damaged in an accident, ensuring your vehicle is restored using manufacturer-approved parts.

Comprehensive Coverage

Comprehensive Kia car insurance protects against damages caused by non-collision incidents such as theft, vandalism, fire, and extreme weather conditions. Given that some Kia models, like the Forte and Soul, are frequently targeted by thieves, comprehensive coverage is a wise investment for added protection.

Hybrid & Electric Car Protection

Kia offers hybrid and electric models such as the Kia EV6 and Niro EV, which come with advanced battery and regenerative braking technology. Specialized Kia insurance ensures these components are covered in the event of an accident, giving hybrid and electric Kia owners peace of mind.

Roadside Assistance
